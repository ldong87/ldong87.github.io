Course project for Fundamentals of Finite Element. I identified shear modulus distribution in an inverse elasticity problem where displacement fields were obtained from ultrasonic imaging. The inverse problem was posed as PDE-constrained minimization, and the finite element method was applied to the Euler-Lagrange equations.

![Measured displacement fields](/images/portfolio/fem-displacement.jpg)
*Input: measured displacement fields in the x and y directions.*

![Recovered shear modulus](/images/portfolio/fem-shear-modulus.jpg)
*Output: recovered shear modulus distribution.*
