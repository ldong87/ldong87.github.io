As a Peter O'Donnell Jr. Postdoctoral Fellow at the Institute for Computational Engineering and Sciences, University of Texas at Austin, my research with Omar Ghattas and Tan Bui focused on physics-based machine learning and inverse problems in electromagnetics and acoustoelastics, with applications in oil exploration.

I maintained a legacy C codebase with more than 1.5 million lines and performed large-scale runs on thousands of CPU cores at TACC.
